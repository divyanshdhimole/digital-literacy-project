-------------------------------
          ✅ DO'S
-------------------------------

1. DO think before you post.
   Ask yourself: "Would I be comfortable if my professor,
   parent, or future employer saw this?" If not, don't post it.

2. DO keep your privacy settings updated.
   Regularly review who can see your posts, stories, and
   personal information on each platform.

3. DO give credit when sharing others' work.
   Always tag or mention the original creator when reposting
   photos, videos, quotes, or ideas.

4. DO use social media to build a positive digital identity.
   Share your achievements, projects, and learning experiences
   to create a professional online presence over time.

5. DO report harmful or false content.
   Use the platform's reporting tools if you come across
   misinformation, cyberbullying, or inappropriate content.

6. DO take regular breaks from social media.
   Set screen time limits to protect your mental health and
   maintain focus on your studies and offline relationships.

-------------------------------
         ❌ DON'TS
-------------------------------

1. DON'T share personal information publicly.
   Avoid posting your phone number, home address, daily
   schedule, or location in real time.

2. DON'T engage in or ignore cyberbullying.
   Do not participate in online harassment, and do not stay
   silent if you witness it happening to others.

3. DON'T spread unverified news or rumours.
   Always check the source before sharing any news, especially
   on sensitive topics like health, politics, or exams.

4. DON'T post content that could embarrass you later.
   Party photos, offensive jokes, or angry rants can affect
   college admissions, scholarships, and job opportunities.

5. DON'T accept friend/follow requests from strangers.
   Unknown accounts can be fake profiles used for scamming,
   phishing, or collecting your personal data.

6. DON'T use social media during class or study hours.
   Constant notifications and scrolling reduce concentration
   and academic performance significantly.

============================================================
   Remember: Your digital footprint is permanent. Build it
   with the same care you would your real-world reputation.
